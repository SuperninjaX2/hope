What we do

protection and Prevention:

Prioritizing the protection of GBV cases and vulnerable children, aiming to prevent, detect, and respond to violence, including sexual and gender-based violence, to ensure victims' safety and well-being.

Empowerment and Support

Core values include empowering survivors of sexual and gender-based violence and vulnerable children, fostering a culture of respect and equality, and promoting universal human rights among young people.

Promoting Holistic Well-being:

The organization prioritizes protecting and empowering vulnerable individuals, including orphans and survivors of gender-based violence, by providing comprehensive support services aimed at improving their overall well-being and reducing vulnerabilities to health risks and discrimination
